About Africa Manufacturing Awards 2024

The Global CEOs Institute and Mikono Expo Group organize the Africa Manufacturing Awards 2024. This premier awards ceremony is dedicated to recognizing and celebrating the remarkable achievements of African manufacturing companies. The event provides a platform for networking, collaboration, and showcasing the best practices in the African manufacturing sector. Open to all manufacturing companies across Africa, regardless of their size or sector; the Africa Manufacturing Awards sets rigorous judging criteria to evaluate participants' excellence, sustainability, growth, and profitability. Emphasizing product quality, innovation, customer service, environmental impact, social responsibility, and good governance, the awards aim to commend and inspire outstanding contributions to the African manufacturing landscape.

Africa Manufacturing Awards

The Africa Manufacturing Awards is a spectacular event offering a wide range of experiences under one roof. Join us for a remarkable journey celebrating excellence in African manufacturing.

Event Highlights

  • Awards Ceremony: Be part of the prestigious Awards Ceremony, where we recognize and honor the outstanding achievements of manufacturing companies across diverse sectors in Africa. Witness industry leaders being celebrated for their exceptional contributions to the manufacturing landscape.
  • Networking Opportunities: Engage in valuable networking sessions, connecting with like-minded professionals, industry experts, and potential partners. Forge strategic relationships that can open doors to collaborations, innovations, and business growth.
  • Keynote Speeches: Immerse yourself in captivating keynote speeches by prominent manufacturing thought leaders and experts. Gain insights into the latest industry trends, innovative technologies, and strategies driving the future of manufacturing in Africa.
  • Inspirational Talks: Be inspired by success stories and experiences shared by manufacturing pioneers. Learn from their journeys, challenges, and triumphs in navigating the ever-evolving manufacturing landscape.
  • Awards Gala: Culminate the event with a grand Awards Gala, a night of celebration and recognition. Witness the announcement of the award winners and join in the festivities as we celebrate their remarkable achievements.
