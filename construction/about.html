About AFRICA CONSTRUCTION AWARDS 2024

The Africa Construction Excellence Awards recognize and celebrate the very best companies, collaborations and projects from across the African region. Totally inclusive, our construction awards are open to the whole sector from buildings to infrastructure, all parts of the supply and demand chain, all disciplines, professions and trades. The Construction Awards of Excellence are truly independent, owned by the sector for the sector, across all of the African continent. Africa’s booming construction industry and features a wide range of categories for solution providers, suppliers, contractors, architects, civil engineers, developers, local authorities and more. The awards are designed to reward achievement for projects, organizations and individuals successfully driving excellence in the industry. The awards are designed to recognize those individuals and companies that set the gold standard in the construction sector. This event is a great opportunity for companies who wish to be seen as proactively leading the way within the construction sector. Winners are selected across 16 categories including Integration, Value, Innovation and our acclaimed Projects of the Year. We bring together a panel of expert judges with significant proven experience in driving and implementing innovation and positive change across the sector. A team of independent experts will pre-check all submitted entries in detail to ensure their validity before the independent expert juries evaluate them. Winners will be announced during the Awards Dinner on the 12th October 2023 from 7pm -10:00pm at Hyatt Regency Dar es Salaam, The Kilimanjaro, and Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Read More
